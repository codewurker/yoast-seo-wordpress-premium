*** Yoast SEO premium Changelog ***


2025-10-07 - version 26.1
* add -  Adds tooltips to the AI Summarize `Generate summary` and `Regenerate summary` buttons when they are disabled.
* add -  Prevents AI Optimize from suggesting improvements to the content of the AI Summarize block.
* add -  Excludes the content of the AI Summarize block from the content that should be summarized when re-generating a summary.
* fix -  Fixes a bug where the modals would be rendered every time on hovering over the transform options in the AI summarize menu.
* update -  Bumps the minimum required version of Yoast SEO to 26.1.
* update -  Adds the `wpseo_premium_orphaned_count_cache_invalidation_method` filter to allow the ability to swap the way to invalidate the `orphaned_count` object cache.
* update -  Adds the `wpseo_premium_orphaned_count_cache` filter to allow the ability to completely disable the `orphaned_count` object cache.
