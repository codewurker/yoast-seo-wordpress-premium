*** Yoast SEO premium Changelog ***

* fix -  Fixes a bug where Table of Content block failed to be registered in Widgets when Twenty Twenty-One theme was used.

* update -  Makes AI Summarize block not available inside Widgets when Twenty Twenty-One theme is used.
